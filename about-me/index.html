About Me

My education and background are artistic but during my career I progressively developed good technical and programming skills.
I believe that these mixed skills is what set me apart.

My artistic education always helped me to get accurate anatomy and an extreme attention to deformations and facial expressions.
On the other hand, the programming skills I have gained, especially in Python and C++, give me an extra boost to solve rigging problems and implement new solutions.
My knowledge in programming is not only useful for rigging. Many times I have been tasked to solve pipeline related problems, such as developing custom file formats for
animation or skins weights, converting motion capture data, communicating with asset management tools and so on.

I might be consider a programmer with an extreme passion for character animation. In reality I enjoy any aspect of the character development.
My ideal job would be one where I'll be able to do as many different things as possible and learn something new in the process.

Specialties:
* Custom tools for rigging and animation
* Animation pipeline
* Developing object oriented automatic rigging systems
